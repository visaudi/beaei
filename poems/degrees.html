degrees

Walls like rivers ruining through the powdery sky

I alone, trying to make it to the next paragraph.

Do you think they like me?
Of course they don't.
Who the fuck are you?
Even when I try, I don't know where to start!
If only we might hear each other, a little.

I hear its thundering flow: loud wrath.
Wrath forged in a precarious bending arc more like a compass than a circuit.

How long shall we wait for this slow motion bending of nightmares and dreams?
We can see the promise so distant from the broken prism chambers of our hearts
hearing the trickling blood so far and so close.
Bitwise wonders shall not wipe you tears away.

If not now what am I?
And if not enough:
nothing?
Those beloved bits inscribed on the smallest parts of our hearts:
may we two just for a short while be together like those tiny moments
when people so far apart
and in so much darkness
uncannily shock us with unconscious love.
